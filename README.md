# fuel

Fuel is a all-in-one solution for tracking your food intake, health, and nutrition. An easy-to-use interface with useful graphs helps you gain oversight
over your journey towards a better life. Being aware of what you eat is the first step to losing weight, and only an effortless logging tool will keep you motivated to stay on track.
